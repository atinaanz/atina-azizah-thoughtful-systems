Curious about systems, technology, and the people they serve.
Hi I'm Atina✦ I'm naturally curious about how things work beneath the surface. Whether it's a product, a workflow, a business process, or a learning system, I enjoy exploring the connections between people, technology, and operations. Across different roles and industries, I've often found myself asking similar questions: Why does this process feel difficult? Where does confusion come from? How can systems better support the people using them? My journey has taken me through software development, content technology, quality analysis, research, and operations. While the roles looked different, they all helped me understand the same thing from different perspectives: how systems are designed, how they behave, and how people experience them. I enjoy bringing structure to complexity, creating documentation that helps knowledge scale, and improving workflows so teams can work with greater clarity and confidence. More than building features, I'm interested in building systems that help people do their best work.
